/ about CORTIA /

CORTIA is a technology company dedicated to transforming Africa's infrastructure through intelligent digital solutions. We develop secure platforms that empower governments, businesses, developers, and institutions to make smarter, faster, and data-driven decisions. Our ecosystem combines geospatial intelligence, land verification, property technology, business compliance, and operational analytics into one connected platform designed for the future. By integrating advanced technologies with local expertise, CORTIA is creating trusted digital infrastructure that drives transparency, efficiency, and sustainable growth across the continent.

Everything You Need to Know About CORTIA

CORTIA is an African technology company building intelligent digital infrastructure solutions that improve how governments, businesses, and communities manage land, verify information, monitor operations, and drive economic growth. Our ecosystem combines innovative technologies to create secure, transparent, and efficient digital services across Africa.

CORTIA offers a growing ecosystem of digital platforms, including LandCerta for land verification, Rental Verify for tenant and property verification, CoreSure for compliance and business verification, OIS for operational intelligence, and Oja for digital commerce. Together, these solutions simplify complex processes and enable smarter decision-making..

Our platforms are designed for governments, businesses, real estate developers, financial institutions, property managers, enterprises, NGOs, and individuals seeking trusted digital infrastructure solutions.
